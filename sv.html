Phone Safety Lab — Camera & Location Demo

This safe demo shows the same permission prompts attackers try to exploit in phishing pages. Nothing leaves your device — data stays in this page only.

1) Camera/Microphone Prompt

Tap Enable Camera. Your browser will ask for permission. If you deny, the camera stream won’t start.

Status: idle

Snapshot preview (stays local).

2) GPS Location Prompt

Tap Get My Location. Your browser will request location access. If you allow, your approximate coordinates will display below.

Status: idle

Tip: In real phishing, pages send these coordinates to a server. This demo does not transmit anything.

How to use (quick)

  1. Open this file in your phone’s browser, or serve it locally with python -m http.server inside its folder.
  2. Press the buttons above to see the exact permission prompts.
  3. Denying permissions stops access; granting shows how data could be read.

Remember: never grant camera/mic/location permissions on unknown websites.
